Café Velodrome: Your Coffee Haven in Madrid

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Madrid, Café Velodrome is more than just a café—it's a haven for those who appreciate the finer things in life: exceptional coffee, delightful pastries, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're seeking a place to unwind with friends or a cozy corner to work, Café Velodrome is the perfect destination to indulge in both comfort and sophistication.

Why Choose Café Velodrome?

1. Prime Location in Madrid’s Center

  • Located just steps away from the city's bustling streets, Café Velodrome offers an inviting retreat from the urban rush. Whether you're shopping along Gran Vía or visiting local attractions, our café provides a perfect pause in the heart of Madrid.

2. Unmatched Coffee Experience

  • At Café Velodrome, we serve more than just your average cup of coffee. With expertly selected beans and a mastery of brewing techniques, each cup is a testament to our commitment to quality. From rich espressos to smooth cappuccinos, our coffee is a true reflection of the rich traditions of Madrid and beyond.

3. Pastries & Delights Made with Love

  • We believe that a great cup of coffee deserves a great companion, which is why our pastry selection is crafted daily with care. From delicate croissants to decadent cakes, each treat is baked to perfection to complement your coffee experience.

4. Comfortable & Elegant Atmosphere

  • Whether you're here to catch up with friends or find some peace in your day, Café Velodrome offers a cozy and elegant ambiance that suits every occasion. With its modern décor, soft lighting, and relaxed atmosphere, you’ll feel right at home as you savor every sip.

5. A Space to Connect

  • Café Velodrome is not just a place to drink coffee—it's a place to connect. Whether you're looking to meet friends, network, or simply enjoy some quiet time, our café is designed to be a space for everyone. We welcome all walks of life, making it a social hub for both locals and visitors alike.

6. Sustainable & Locally Sourced Ingredients

  • We take pride in supporting local farmers and suppliers. Our coffee beans, pastries, and other ingredients are sourced responsibly to ensure that every bite and every sip supports not only the environment but also the community.
