What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, mobile, or tablet) by your web browser. They serve various essential functions by enabling our site to recognize your device and capture and remember certain information. Cookies help create a smooth browsing experience by allowing our website to function more effectively, remember your preferences, and facilitate tailored advertising where applicable.

Cookies can be categorized into different types based on their purpose:

  • Essential Cookies: These are vital for the operation of our website, allowing you to navigate and use key functionalities. Without these cookies, certain services would be unavailable.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors interact with our website, help us understand visitor behavior, and allow us to improve the performance and structure of our site.
  • Functional Cookies: Functional cookies remember your preferences and choices, providing a more personalized experience as you browse.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertising that is tailored to your interests. They can track your browsing habits and behavior across various websites to display relevant ads.
